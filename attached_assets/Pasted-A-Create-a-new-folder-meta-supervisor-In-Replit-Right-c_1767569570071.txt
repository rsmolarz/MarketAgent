3. Where each new component fits in your system

Here is the mental model, mapped to your repo:

scheduler.py
   |
   |-- run_with_telemetry(...)     ← telemetry/context.py
   |
   |-- _run_agent()
        |
        |-- agent.run()
        |
        |-- emit_alpha_signal()    ← alpha/emit.py
        |
        |-- store findings


Separately:

meta_supervisor/
   |
   |-- build_meta_report.py
         |
         |-- reads telemetry/events.jsonl
         |-- reads alpha/events.jsonl
         |-- reads eval/results/*
         |
         |-- outputs meta_supervisor/reports/meta_report.json


Then:

GitHub Actions
   |
   |-- pr_comment.py         ← comments on PR
   |-- promotion_gate.py    ← creates PR sandbox → prod


Nothing here interferes with runtime execution.

6. Hard truth (important)

What you are building is not part of the app.

It is:

That is why it lives next to your app, not inside services/ or routes/.
